Simon Game

In this project, players are presented with a sequence of colors and sounds, which they must then replicate by pressing corresponding buttons. The catch? With each successful round, the sequence becomes longer and more challenging, putting your memory to the ultimate test. Whether you're competing against yourself to beat your high score or challenging friends to see who can remember the longest sequence, the addictive nature of this game will keep you coming back for more.
